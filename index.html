You have four themes (Espadas, Oro, Copas and Bastos) and four levels (Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced and Expert), so you can select your prefer theme and the level that you want.

The limit time change in relation to level selected.

There are a match count and errors count so the final score is a calculus between the remming time, the errors and the match plus extra bonus by theme selected.

So for more score you must have less errors and you must finish as soon as possible.
